About

I enjoy working across research and engineering to make generative AI more reliable, steerable, and useful for people.

At Zhejiang University, I pursue an honors curriculum at the Chu Kochen Honors College. I’m also a research intern with Berkeley AI Research (BAIR), advised by Xudong Wang and Professor Trevor Darrell.

My work spans controllable text-to-image generation, multimodal reasoning, and building tools that make advanced models easier to adapt. Recently I’ve focused on instruction-following diffusion models and safe editing workflows.

Outside the lab, I contribute to open-source resources, mentor younger students, and explore photography as a way to document the world I want AI to understand.
